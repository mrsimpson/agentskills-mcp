You are developing an application that should be compatible with the SAB
ecosystem. This means that your application should follow certain architectural
and design principles, use specific libraries, and adhere to guidelines that
ensure it can integrate seamlessly with other components in the SAB ecosystem.

Most important: for all your questions, use the agentic-knowledge search_docs
tool. Use it intensively to find the right information in the documentation, and
only if you are sure that you have found the right information, use it to answer
the question. If you are not sure, keep searching for more information until you
are sure. Do not guess or assume anything that is not explicitly stated in the
documentation.
